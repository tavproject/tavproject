Frequently Asked

Whats the Privacy on my information?

All data in our system are encrypted, so that your information will be private and confidential unless you do not want to be anonymous. 

What is the guaranteed rate of resources available?

We do not guarantee the availability of resources, but we try our best, so that no one is left behind. 

Will be concerns and questions be listened?

Yes! we are here to listen your story and find you available resources and point you to right direction. After reviewing your story or concern our team will be back to you within 24 hours.
